ESPN

Description

ESPN.com is one of my most visited sites. It is the source of all my information regarding sports. Not only do I like ESPN because of its content, its well-designed layout also contributes to my love for the site. With automatic updates to scores located on the top of the page and interactive subboxes located at the bottom, there is always something to read and something to be updated with when on this site. The red color scheme is something that I can never get tired of and i have actually become extremely used to it. The site also has the main headline for a day with a corresponding picture right on the front page making my visit very convenient. THe pages are all consistent, with navigation on the top bar and resources on the right side so I won't get lost. In addition, transitions are smooth and images and text are clear and straightforward so I won't have to question anything, ever.
